# Spotify Listening Experience — Research Survey

A questionnaire designed to (a) sort each respondent into one of the **six audience
segments** from the review analysis and (b) validate the **six research questions**
and the key themes (ads, shuffle/repeat, recommendations, missing features).

**Two ways to create the Google Form:**
1. **Automatic (recommended):** open [script.google.com](https://script.google.com), paste
   `spotify_segment_survey.gs`, run `buildSpotifySurvey`, approve permissions — the log
   prints the live Form URL.
2. **Manual:** copy the questions below into a blank Google Form
   ([forms.new](https://forms.new)).

---

## How questions map to segments & research questions

| Section | Purpose | Segments / Questions it serves |
|---|---|---|
| 1. About You | Segmentation | Classifies into all 6 segments |
| 2. Discovering New Music | Q1 discovery | All segments |
| 3. Repetition & Control | Q2 repeat listening | Programmed Listener (top: Shuffle/Repeat) |
| 4. Recommendations | Q3 frustrations | Super, Moderate, Programmed |
| 5. How & Why You Listen | Q5 behaviours | All; esp. Programmed & Moderate |
| 6. Frustrations & Wishes | Q6 unmet needs | Free Tier (ads), all (Missing Features) |

**Segmentation cues (from Section 1):**
- **Free Tier User** → "Free (with ads)" plan *(most affected, 12.5% neg — ads)*
- **Super Listener** → 3+ years + uses Wrapped / Discover Weekly / Family plan
- **Previously Active** → cancelled or considered leaving / switched platform
- **Light Listener** → < 6 months, rare use
- **Programmed Listener** → "let autoplay, radio, or shuffle decide"
- **Moderate Listener** → regular daily user, picks own music *(default bucket)*

## Section 1 — About You

1. **How long have you used Spotify?** *(multiple choice, required)*
   Less than 1 month / 1–6 months / 6 months – 1 year / 1–3 years / More than 3 years / I no longer use it
2. **What plan are you currently on?** *(required)*
   Free (with ads) / Premium Individual / Premium Duo or Family / Premium Student / I cancelled / no longer subscribe
3. **How often do you listen to Spotify?** *(required)*
   Multiple times a day / About once a day / A few times a week / Rarely
4. **Which best describes how you usually start listening?** *(required)*
   I search for and pick specific songs/artists/playlists / I let autoplay, radio, or shuffle decide / An even mix of both
5. **Which Spotify features do you regularly use?** *(checkboxes, required)*
   Discover Weekly / Release Radar / Daily Mix / Wrapped / Blend / Liked Songs / Playlists you created / Autoplay or Radio / None
6. **Have you ever cancelled Premium or seriously considered leaving Spotify?** *(required)*
   Yes — cancelled / Yes — considered / No
7. **If you left or considered leaving, where did you go (or consider going)?** *(checkboxes)*
   Apple Music / YouTube Music / Tidal / Amazon Music / Stopped paid streaming / Not applicable

## Section 2 — Discovering New Music *(Q1)*

8. **How satisfied are you with how Spotify helps you discover new music?** *(scale 1–5: Very dissatisfied → Very satisfied)*
9. **How often do you find genuinely new artists or songs you love through Spotify?** *(Often / Sometimes / Rarely / Never)*
10. **What gets in the way of discovering new music?** *(checkboxes + Other)*
    Recommendations feel repetitive / Suggestions don't match my taste / Too much focus on what I already listen to / Discovery features hard to navigate / App bugs or performance / I don't have trouble
11. **Describe a time Spotify recommended something that worked really well — or really didn't.** *(long answer)*

## Section 3 — Repetition & Control *(Q2)*

12. **How often does it feel like Spotify plays the same songs over and over?** *(scale 1–5: Never → Constantly)*
13. **When does repetition bother you most?** *(checkboxes)*
    On shuffle / In autoplay or radio / In algorithmic playlists (Daily Mix) / Even in my own playlists / Doesn't bother me
14. **I feel in control of what plays next.** *(scale 1–5: Strongly disagree → Strongly agree)*

## Section 4 — Recommendations *(Q3)*

15. **Spotify's recommendations match my taste.** *(scale 1–5)*
16. **I feel Spotify keeps me in a "bubble" of very similar music.** *(scale 1–5)*
17. **What frustrates you most about recommendations?** *(checkboxes + Other)*
    Too repetitive / Too random / Ignores my mood or context / Pushes podcasts or audiobooks / Can't influence or reset them / Nothing — they work well

## Section 5 — How & Why You Listen *(Q5)*

18. **In which situations do you mainly listen?** *(checkboxes + Other, required)*
    Focus/work/study / Workout / Commute or driving / Relaxing, sleep, background / Mood or emotional / Social or parties
19. **In those moments, what are you mainly trying to get from Spotify?** *(long answer)*
20. **How well does Spotify serve your main listening situation?** *(scale 1–5: Very poorly → Very well)*

## Section 6 — Frustrations & Wishes *(Q6 + ads / missing features)*

21. **How disruptive are ads to your listening?** *(required)*
    I'm on Premium / Not disruptive / Somewhat / Very / So disruptive it makes me consider leaving
22. **Which features do you most wish Spotify had — or brought back?** *(checkboxes + Other)*
    Better queue control / Truly random shuffle / Better search and filtering / More control over recommendations / Lyrics improvements / Cheaper or flexible plans / Hi-fi audio / Fewer or shorter ads
23. **What is the single biggest thing Spotify could fix or add for you?** *(long answer, required)*
24. **How likely are you to recommend Spotify to a friend?** *(scale 0–10 — NPS)*
25. **Anything else you'd like to tell us about your Spotify experience?** *(long answer)*
